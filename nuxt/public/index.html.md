# Flesh and Bones

Flesh and Bones is a digital creative agency website built around a highly visual presentation of portfolio work, represented directors, agency philosophy, and contact information.

## What this page does

The homepage introduces the agency, presents featured work, and routes visitors toward the most important sections of the site.

## Best next pages

- `/work` for the broader project portfolio
- `/directors` for represented talent
- `/manifesto` for the agency point of view
- `/contact` for founders, locations, and contact pathways

## Notes for agents

- The homepage is useful for orientation, but it is not the full source of portfolio or contact detail.
- The site uses CMS-driven content and media-heavy layouts, so follow the section pages for better structured information.
