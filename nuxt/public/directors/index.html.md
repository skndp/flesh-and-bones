# Directors

The directors page is the public directory of represented directors for Flesh and Bones.

## What this page does

It acts as an index of talent and links to individual director profile pages under `/directors/{slug}`.

## What a director profile typically includes

- Director name
- Tagline
- Reel or featured video
- Biography or anatomy section
- Location
- Tags or specialties
- Related projects

## Notes for agents

- Use this section for talent discovery and attribution.
- For a specific director, prefer the individual profile page over the index when available.
